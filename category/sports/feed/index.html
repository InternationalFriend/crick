This World Cup winning footballer won the ‘FIFA The Best’ award after a hard-fought battle.

Arling Haaland and Messi had equal votes. However, Messi became the first as he was ahead of the national team captains in the first choice list. Not only the captain, but also the coaches of the national teams voted for the player of the year. Media representatives from different countries also voted for the player of the year.

Bangladesh national football team captain Jamal Bhuiyan and coach Javier Cabrera also voted for the best player. Jamal topped Messi’s best male player poll. Jamal’s third best player voted for Holland was City star Kevin De Bruyne.

Jamal placed Messi on top of all, but Messi is not in the top three of Spanish coach Cabrera of Bangladesh. In the eyes of the Spanish coach, Holland is the best footballer in 2023. Cabrera’s next two votes also went to the Premier League and Champions League winning City players’ box. Cabrera’s second best is Rodri, third best is Ilkay Gundwan.

In addition, in the selection of the best players of the women’s team, Bangladesh women’s team captain Sabina Khatun’s preferred order was Hinata Miyazawa, Sam Carr and Alex Greenwood. None of them made it to the top three. Saiful Bari, the coach of the women’s team, was voted the best female footballer in the box of Aitana Bonmati.
