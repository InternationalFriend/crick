Dhaka: No one has died due to corona virus in the country in the last 24 hours. So far 29 thousand 477 people have died due to Corona in the country.Six people were newly identified on this day. In all, the number of infected people stands at 20 lakh 45 thousand 998.

This information was informed in a press release about Corona sent by the Department of Health on Monday (November 13) afternoon.    

It is said that nine people including asymptomatic patients have recovered in the last 24 hours in various hospitals and homes of the country including Dhaka City. A total of 20 lakh 13 thousand 692 people have recovered so far. 556 samples have been tested in the last 24 hours in 885 labs under public and private management across the country. So far 1 crore 56 lakh six thousand 469 samples have been tested.

In the notification, the detection rate in the sample test in the last 24 hours is 1.08 percent. So far, the detection rate considering the sample test is 13.11 percent. The recovery rate is 98.42 percent. The mortality rate for detection is 1.44 percent.

It further informed that one person has come to isolation in the last 24 hours and no one has been discharged from isolation. So far 4 lakh 52 thousand 827 people have come under isolation and 4 lakh 23 thousand 590 people have received clearance from isolation. Currently 29 thousand 237 people are in isolation.  

According to the information of the Department of Health, the first patient of corona virus was detected in the country on March 8, 2020. 10 days later, on March 18, the first person died due to corona.
